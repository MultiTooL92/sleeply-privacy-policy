Privacy Policy for Sleeply

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Sleeply ("the app") is developed for Android devices. This policy explains what data is collected when you use it.

1. Data Sleeply collects directly

Sleeply does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers, and does not transmit your usage data to its developer. All app settings (timer duration, fade-out length, default audio player, toggles) are stored only on your device using local app storage and are never uploaded anywhere.

2. Advertising

Sleeply is free to use and supported by ads served through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data such as your advertising ID, device information, approximate location, and ad interaction data, in order to show ads and measure their performance. This processing is governed by Google's own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the UK, Sleeply shows a consent form (via Google's User Messaging Platform) the first time you open the app, letting you choose whether to allow personalized advertising. You can change this choice at any time from Settings > Ad privacy options.

3. Permissions Sleeply uses

  • Foreground service / notification: lets the countdown keep running and shows its progress while the app is in the background. No data leaves your device for this.
  • Accessibility service: used only to turn off your screen when the countdown reaches zero. Sleeply does not read, record, or act on anything else shown on your screen.
  • Do Not Disturb access (optional): only requested if you turn on "Mute notifications and calls" in Settings, to silence notifications and calls while the countdown is active.
  • Notifications: used to show the ongoing countdown status.

4. Data sharing

Sleeply does not sell or share personal data with third parties, other than the advertising data described above, which is collected directly by Google's AdMob SDK under its own privacy policy.

5. Children's privacy

Sleeply is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the app changes. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
